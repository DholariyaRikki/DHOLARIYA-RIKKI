Our Story

A LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE

Heritage & Vision

Founded in 1925, Serenity Grand has been the epitome of luxury hospitality for nearly a century. Our commitment to excellence has made us a destination for discerning travelers worldwide.

We blend timeless elegance with modern sophistication, creating an experience that honors our heritage while embracing innovation.

Architectural Marvel

Our property showcases stunning architecture that seamlessly blends classical grandeur with contemporary design. Every corner tells a story of craftsmanship and attention to detail.

From the grand lobby to intimate garden spaces, each area is designed to create unforgettable moments and lasting memories.
